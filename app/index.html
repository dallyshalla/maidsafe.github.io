This is the Internet the way it was supposed to be.

The SAFE (Secure Access For Everyone) Network is the key to a world of new and exciting apps where security, privacy and freedom are already built in. Experience the joy of storing and sharing data that only you control. Speak and communicate with family and friends in complete privacy.

Your data is always private and secure!

Rather than using data centres and servers, which are prone to data theft and shopping, the SAFE Network uses advanced P2P technology, that is comprised of the spare computing resources of other SAFE users.

This crowdsourced Internet means that you, and only you, control your data at all times. Using SAFE apps will enable you to share data of any type or size on an open network not owned or controlled by anyone.

The service that pays you!

By providing your unused computing resources to SAFE you will be paid in a network token, called safecoin (HYPERLINK TO SAFECOIN PAGE).

All that you require to do to start earning is to download the free SAFE software, create your account and tell the network how much space you are going to provide. It’s that simple! Overtime, your online wallet will start receive safecoin, which you can use to pay for other services on the network, or convert to another currency via an SAFE currency exchange.
